Visual Identity

When people view my portfolio, I want them to think I am a colourful, entheusiastic person who enjoys experimenting with layouts and interactvity. I want my portfolio to be very representative of the colours and fonts I like, as well as my technical abilities in HTML, CSS, JS, and the software I use in daily life such as Adobe Creative Cloud and Figma.
